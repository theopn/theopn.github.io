## My Dotfiles

[*GitHub Repository ⧉*](https://github.com/theopn/dotfiles)

Well, does this count as a project? But this is the repository I spend most of my time committing for sure. This repository includes configurations for text editors like Vim, Neovim, and Emacs, zsh, kitty, tmux, and other terminal/shell related programs, i3 & Polybar and Sway & Waybar, and weirdos like Mutt and w3m.

## i3 & Sway Tips

[*GitHub Repository ⧉*](https://github.com/theopn/i3-sway-tips)

Collection of commands, keybindings, and recommended applications for i3 or Sway window manager beginners. Some of the recommendations are subjective and does not contain all the information you might need, but I would love to make this a community project so feel free to suggest or correct information through issues or to me personally!
