About Me

Hi! My name is Theo, and I do… a lot of different things.⌗

Academics⌗

I am a student at Purdue University studying computer science and philosophy. When I was in middle school, I would collect ISO files for old operating systems, (shout out to WinWorld), install them on virtual machines, and be fascinated by not only the OS themselves but the installation processes. While I did not have a coding background besides a little bit of C, I knew I loved playing with computers. This turns out to be true, and I enjoy studying computer science. My favorite courses include CS18000: OOP, CS182: Foundations of CS (discrete math mostly), and CS240: C Programming. However, my all-time favorite is MA351: Linear Algebra with Professor Sinclair.

I also like writing and math, particularly logic and discrete math, which helped me major in philosophy along with the influence of my dad.

Outside of the school work, I am a member of Undergraduate Student Board for the CS department.

Career⌗

My Résumé ⧉

My love for teaching is reflected in my past jobs. I worked as an undergraduate teaching assistant for CS18000: Problem Solving and Objected-Oriented Programming from the second semester of the freshman year to the following summer. I also worked as a course content developer during the same summer, developing homework assignments and auto-grading programs for CS180 using the JUnit framework.

Throughout the 2022 - 2023 school year, I worked as an instructor for CS19300: Tools. CS193 is an awesome classs where computer science, data science, and artificial intelligence majors of Purdue learn fundamental CS tools such as Git, *nix shell commands, and Vim. I managed 437 students across three sections, and it was an awesome experience directly communicating with freshman and managing a big course.

More about me⌗

I love tiling window managers. My favorite is i3/Sway for its simplicity and Vim style keybindings. I am currently working on a project to make AwesomeWM a full desktop environment with completed Wibar, layout, and integration with other programs like Vim. Meanwhile, you can checkout my i3 and Sway configurations (along with Vim, Neovim, Emacs, Mutt, Zsh etc) in my dotfiles repository. I use Fedora, btw.

I love taking photos. I own Canon EOS M50 with EF-M 15-45mm f/3.5-6.3 lens, and I have taken many portraits with it. Maybe one day I will share my photos online.

I am colorblind.

I collect, modify, and make mechanical watches. If you want to take a peek at my collection (which the Seiko NH movement makes up 80% of), let me know.

I love writing, despite being not particularly good at it.

I love organization and cleaning.

I love salmon.
